How It Works

Instead of memorizing digits — memorize a pattern. Your PIN is hidden among 77 characters in a grid. Only you know where: "my PIN is under the highlighted circles" or "first column top to bottom."

Even if someone sees your screen, they won't know which digits are your actual PIN code.

Secure

Simple

One glance and you remember the code. No complex passwords.
