Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 16, 2025

Introduction

PIN Master ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application PIN Master (the "App").

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

PIN Master is designed with privacy as a core principle. All your data (PIN cards, grid configurations, settings) is stored locally on your device and, if you choose to enable iCloud sync, in your personal iCloud account.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in the following locations:

  • Local Storage: All app data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS data storage mechanisms.
  • iCloud (Optional): If you have iCloud enabled, your data may be synced across your devices via your personal iCloud account. This data is encrypted and managed by Apple according to their privacy policy.

We never have access to your data. It remains entirely within your control on your devices and Apple's iCloud infrastructure.

Analytics

We use TelemetryDeck, a privacy-focused analytics service, to collect anonymous usage statistics. This helps us understand how the app is used and improve it.

TelemetryDeck collects:

  • Anonymous usage events (e.g., "card created", "settings opened")
  • App version and iOS version
  • Device type (e.g., iPhone, iPad)

TelemetryDeck does NOT collect:

  • Personal information
  • Your PIN codes or card data
  • Precise location
  • Advertising identifiers

TelemetryDeck is GDPR-compliant and does not track individual users. For more information, see TelemetryDeck's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple iCloud: For optional data synchronization across your devices. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • TelemetryDeck: For anonymous analytics as described above.

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is stored using iOS secure storage mechanisms
  • iCloud data is encrypted by Apple
  • We do not transmit your PIN codes or card data to any server
  • The App supports Face ID and Touch ID for additional protection

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
